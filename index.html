About Us

The Modern Veg was founded in 2014 by Caitlin Monstero as a way to help people who are new to plant based cooking find inspiration and confidence in the kitchen. We host a variety of different classes, community events, and pop-up dinners around the Twin Cities. At The Modern Veg, we believe anyone can be a great cook, they just need the right tools, ingredients and teacher.
